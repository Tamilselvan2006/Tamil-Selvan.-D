About BGMI

BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) is a multiplayer battle royale game developed by Krafton exclusively for Indian players. It offers fast-paced 100-player battles with various modes like Classic, Arena, Payload, and more. With a strong community and regular updates, it has become one of the top gaming titles in India.

Features

High-Quality Graphics

Enjoy immersive visuals with Ultra HD settings powered by Unreal Engine 4.

Custom Game Modes

Experience various exciting modes including Zombie, Payload, and Team Deathmatch.

Indian Events

Participate in exclusive Indian-themed events and earn exciting rewards.

Game Modes

Classic Mode

Traditional 100-player survival mode with maps like Erangel, Miramar, and Livik.

Arena Mode

Fast-paced 4v4 battles in maps like Warehouse and Hangar.

Payload Mode

Use helicopters and rocket launchers in a warzone setting.

Weapons

Assault Rifles

AKM, M416, Scar-L, Groza and more.

Sniper Rifles

K98k, M24, AWM - deadly from a distance.

Throwables

Frag Grenade, Smoke, Molotov for tactical play.

Maps

Erangel

The most iconic battleground with towns, forests, and military bases.

Miramar

A desert-themed map with long-range gunfights and open terrain.

Livik

A small, fast-paced map perfect for quick matches.
